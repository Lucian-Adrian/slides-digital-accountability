Bias Discovered and Scrapped

Discovery of the Problem

Amazon's AI hiring tool was found to systematically downrank women's resumes for technical positions

The algorithm learned gender bias from historical data where male applicants dominated tech roles

Real Human Impact

Qualified female candidates were automatically filtered out before human review

Resume submissions containing words like "women's" (e.g., "women's chess club") were penalized

Outcome

Amazon abandoned the AI recruitment tool completely after discovering the bias

However, the bias had already affected an unknown number of real job applicants during its use
