Case Study

UK Post Office Horizon

How faulty software led to one of Britain's largest miscarriages of justice

The Scandal & Its Impact

System Errors Blamed on Humans
Horizon accounting software reported phantom shortfalls that were actually caused by system bugs and glitches
Mass Prosecutions
Over 700 subpostmasters were prosecuted between 1999-2015, many imprisoned, forced into bankruptcy, or financially ruined
Blind Trust in Data
Post Office executives and courts trusted the computer system over human testimony for nearly two decades
Convictions Overturned
Convictions began being quashed in 2019 after legal battle, leading to public inquiry and compensation claims
