Transparency Obligations

The EU AI Act requires companies to lift the veil on their AI systems, establishing clear accountability mechanisms.

Documentation Requirements

Companies must maintain detailed technical documentation explaining how their AI systems work and make decisions.

Human Oversight

High-risk AI systems must include human supervision capabilities, allowing operators to intervene, override, or disable the system when necessary.

Risk Management

Companies must implement risk assessment procedures and take measures to mitigate potential harms throughout the AI system lifecycle.
