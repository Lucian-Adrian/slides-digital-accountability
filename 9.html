No Villain, Just Compliance

When responsibility diffuses across a chain of actors, accountability becomes invisible

Politicians

"We're protecting kids."

Create laws with noble goals but questionable methods, then pass implementation responsibility downward

Companies

"We're following the law."

Claim they have no choice but to comply, then delegate the technical implementation to developers

Developers

"We're just implementing the feature."

Build the surveillance tools while distancing themselves from policy implications and consequences
