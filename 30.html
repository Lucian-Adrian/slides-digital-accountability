The EU AI Act: Introducing Risk Categories

The new law categorizes AI systems based on their potential harm, with clear regulatory boundaries

Unacceptable Risk
Social scoring systems that rank citizens
Biometric categorization based on sensitive traits
Emotion recognition in workplaces and educational settings
Untargeted scraping of facial images from the internet
High Risk
Critical infrastructure management systems
Educational assessment and training tools
Employment and worker management systems
Law enforcement AI and biometric identification
Effective: August 2024
