Victim Recourse

For the first time, people harmed by software have a clear path to compensation under EU law

Victims can claim compensation if faulty software causes physical, material or psychological harm
Code now has legal consequences and companies must answer for their software's impacts
Burden of proof is eased for victims through documentation access and presumptions of defect

Real Impact Recognition

"The revised PLD acknowledges what was always true: software affects real lives. When it fails, real people suffer, and they deserve compensation just as with physical products."

Digital Accountability | October 2025
