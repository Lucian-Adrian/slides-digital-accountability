Product Liability Directive: Expansion

Revised in 2024: Software and digital products now officially classified as "products", For the first time, EU law explicitly recognizes that digital code can cause physical harm and financial damage just like physical products

Key Changes

• Software applications, operating systems, and AI systems now legally classified as products
• Both standalone software and embedded systems included in scope
• Digital services with tangible impact fall under the same liability standards
