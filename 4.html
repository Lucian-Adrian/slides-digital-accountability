The EU's CSAR Proposal

The Child Sexual Abuse Regulation would require scanning all private communications — a surveillance approach unprecedented in democratic societies

Private Messages Scanned

Personal conversations would be automatically scanned before they reach the recipient — even in end-to-end encrypted apps

Photos & Videos Scanned

All media files in messages would be analyzed by automated detection systems, flagging potential concerns

URLs & Links Scanned

Web addresses shared in conversations would be checked against databases of flagged content

Encrypted Chats Not Protected

Scanning would happen on your device before encryption — effectively creating a backdoor in secure communications
